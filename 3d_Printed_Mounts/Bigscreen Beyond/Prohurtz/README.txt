You will need:
	2x 130 degree cameras. It does not support other cameras due to size: https://www.aliexpress.us/item/3256804332354572.html
	2x 30mm round 1mm thick IR cut filters for this mod: https://www.aliexpress.us/item/3256802552685824.html Any filter that matches those specs should work.
	a small bit of hotglue to fully secure everything

EXPECT THIS MOUNT TO TOUCH YOUR EYELASHES
if you have a thin interface, or are sensitive, this mount likely will not work well for you.
